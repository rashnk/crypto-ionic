error 4)  : route params not changing when tab change
cause : in router/index.js  path: 'tab3/:id', router.push('tabs/tab3/101')
solution : use named path, in router/index.js  
          {path: 'tab2',name:'tab3'}
          router.push({name:'tab3',params:{id:101}})
          https://forum.ionicframework.com/t/ionic-vue-switch-tab-from-another-tab/209930/2
